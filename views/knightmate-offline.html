Knightmate

A chess variant where you must checkmate the Knight instead of the King

Rules:

The rules are the same as normal chess, with the following exceptions:

  • The two Kings are replaced with Knights
  • The four Knights are replaced with Kings
  • The Knights are the pieces that hold "royal power", a.k.a. can be put in check/checkmate
  • The Kings can be captured like normal pieces
  • Pawns can promote to King, but not Knight
  • Castling works normally, but with a Knight instead of a King

NOTE: Currently, pawns auto-promote to a Queen. I will add a selection to choose which piece to promote soon.
